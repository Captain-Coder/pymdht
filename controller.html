The controller module is designed to be the central point where most modules are connected. This module delegates most of the implementation details to other modules. This delegation model creates separated responsibility areas where implementation can be changed in isolation.

The extreme cases are the plug-ins which allow us to develop/run different implementations of routing and lookup managers in parallel.
